Activities & Excursions

Discover the wonders of our tropical paradise and create unforgettable memories with our curated selection of activities and excursions.

Relaxation & Rejuvenation

  • Sunrise Yoga: Peaceful start to your day. (Complimentary)
  • Guided Meditation: Daily sessions in our serene garden.
  • Beachside Lounging: Enjoy our private beach and umbrellas.
  • Infinity Pool: Ocean views and pure relaxation.

Adventure & Recreation

  • Snorkeling: Coral reefs & marine life. (Fees apply)
  • Kayaking: Explore hidden coves. (Rentals available)
  • SUP Boarding: Fun workout on water. (Rentals available)
  • Beach Volleyball: Daily games by the sea.
  • Fitness Center: Open 24/7 with top equipment.
  • Kids' Club: Supervised daily activities for all ages.

Explore the Natural Beauty

  • Reef Snorkeling Tour: Half-day guided experience.
  • Island Hopping: Full-day scenic boat adventure.
  • Rainforest Hike: See waterfalls and wildlife.
  • Sunset Cruise: Romantic ocean cruise. (Evening only)

Cultural Experiences

  • Village Visit: Explore local traditions.
  • Cooking Class: Learn to prepare local dishes.
  • Live Performance: Traditional music & dance.

Adventure & Wildlife

  • Diving Excursions: Multiple dive sites, certified instructors.
  • Wildlife Sanctuary: Meet exotic animals nearby.
  • Deep-Sea Fishing: Half or full-day adventures.

Booking & Information

  • Visit our concierge to book activities.
  • Check availability & prices at the front desk.
  • Transportation available upon request.
  • We recommend early bookings in peak season.
