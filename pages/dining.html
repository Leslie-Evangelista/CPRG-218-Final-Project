Dining

A Culinary Journey

Indulge in a diverse range of dining experiences at Shangri La Beach Resort. From elegant fine dining to casual beachfront bites, our restaurants and bars offer something to tantalize every taste bud and create unforgettable culinary moments.

The Beach House – Fine Dining with Ocean Views

Experience the epitome of culinary excellence at The Beach House. Enjoy panoramic ocean views, fresh seafood, and impeccable service in an elegant ambiance.

  • Cuisine: Fine Dining, Seafood, International
  • Ambiance: Elegant, Romantic, Oceanfront
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Reservations: Recommended

The Sand Bar – Relaxed Beachfront Grill

Enjoy delicious grilled favorites and refreshing beachside eats with your feet in the sand. Perfect for casual meals by the ocean.

  • Cuisine: Casual, Grill, Seafood, American
  • Ambiance: Relaxed, Beachfront, Informal
  • Dress Code: Casual
  • Reservations: Not Required

The Poolside Cafe – Refreshments and Light Bites

Stay refreshed with wraps, salads, snacks, and beverages without leaving the pool. Ideal for quick and tasty bites under the sun.

  • Cuisine: Casual, Light Bites, Snacks, Beverages
  • Ambiance: Relaxed, Poolside, Informal
  • Dress Code: Casual
  • Reservations: Not Required

The Lobby Lounge – Sophisticated Cocktails & Ambiance

Unwind with fine wines, handcrafted cocktails, and appetizers in a refined space with live music on select evenings.

  • Cuisine: Beverages, Light Appetizers
  • Ambiance: Sophisticated, Relaxed, Live Music (select evenings)
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Reservations: Not Required

Other Dining Options

  • Room Service: Extensive menu available 24/7 in the comfort of your room.
  • Buffet Breakfast: Wide variety of hot/cold dishes, fruits, and pastries.
  • Special Dietary Options: We accommodate dietary needs and allergies – just let us know.
